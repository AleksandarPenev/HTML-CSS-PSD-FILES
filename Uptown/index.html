Uptown Fayetteville

Enjoy all the character of Fayetteville without the noise and traffic of campus. Uptown Fayetteville introduces modern, green apartments, restaurants, and shops in a walkable neighborhood. Award-winning architecture sets Uptown apart from other apartments in Northwest Arkansas. One and two-bedroom apartments—some with spiral stairs leading to private rooftop decks—will be available this winter.

Uptown borders a protected woodland and creek, and the community is designed as an extension of the Razorback Greenway trail system, with a rooftop garden, courtyards, and water features to bring neighbors together. Restaurants, coffee, movies, and shops are all in walking distance, and downtown and the Dickson Street entertainment district are a quick drive or bike ride away.

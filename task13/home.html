Bake Eat Love

Premium artisanal pastries, lunch and coffee.

Our food is locally sourced, handcrafted and baked fresh daily without the use of additives or preservatives.

Pastries

A selection of expertly handcrafted pastries made with real butter and premium ingredients baked fresh every day. Favourites include almond croissants, seasonal danish and ham & cheese croissants.

Lunch

Available daily at 11am.

Inspired by the Fraser Valley’s produce
and meats, sandwiches on house made bread rotate through the seasons. Wholesome soups and salads are periodically featured as part of a select menu – check out our social media for availability.

Beverages

Our custom roasted beans are
thoughtfully crafted by Republica Coffee Roasters in Fort Langley, so whether it’s an Americano, a cappuccino or a latte paired with one of our house made syrups,
we’ve got you covered. Seasonal flavours, teas and juices are also available.

Treat yo'self

We decided not to stop at just croissants and danish. Delectable menu items
include lemon sugar buns, cookies, bars, cinnamon buns and sticky buns to name a few. Don’t miss our Saturday line up featuring a wide array of glazed and filled doughnuts.
